About Us

AgroFirst is an AgroTech starthub with the sole aim of addressing the challenges faced by Farmers in the sub-saharan west African countries.

These challenges includes:

  • Market Place
  • Networking
  • Capital

AgroFirst provides a platform where farmers can display their goods and ideas to an unimaginable large market of buyers and investors
